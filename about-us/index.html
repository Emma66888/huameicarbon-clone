About Us

Home / About Us

Huamei Activated Carbon Company from China

Huamei Carbon has more than 20 years of experience in manufacturing activated carbon. We produce granular activated carbon, pellet activated carbon, powder activated carbon with anthracite coal, bituminous coal, coconut shell, and wood as raw material. With many production lines and advanced technology, we have become the leading activated carbon manufacturer and supplier in China.

Our activated carbon has been certified by ISO, Kosher, Halal, and SGS. Our technicians and sales team will give you the best solution for your project. Since the establishment of our company we have served 1000+ customers and have produced for them the most suitable activated carbon for their projects.
