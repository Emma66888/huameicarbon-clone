Honeycomb Activated Carbon

Home / Products / Honeycomb Activated Carbon

Overview

Honeycomb Activated Carbon is a monolithic structured carbon material with thousands of parallel micro-channels, offering ultra-low pressure drop and high mass transfer efficiency. Available in both carbon-based and ceramic-supported formulations, it is the preferred choice for continuous gas-phase purification systems.

Its unique honeycomb structure maximizes geometric surface area while minimizing resistance to airflow, making it ideal for large-volume VOC abatement, industrial odor control, and catalyst support applications. Custom cell densities from 50 to 400 cpsi (cells per square inch) are available.

Ultra-low pressure drop: 1/10 of packed bed
Cell density: 50 - 400 cpsi
Carbon-based & ceramic coated options
Custom sizes: block, cylinder, panel

Product Features

Why choose our honeycomb activated carbon

Ultra-Low Pressure Drop

Straight channel design reduces airflow resistance by up to 90% compared to granular packed beds, saving energy in continuous systems.

High Geometric Area

Honeycomb structure provides large geometric surface area per unit volume, enhancing mass transfer efficiency and adsorption kinetics.

High Temperature Resistance

Ceramic-based versions withstand up to 650°C, suitable for high-temperature exhaust and catalytic oxidation processes.

Regenerable & Reusable

Can be thermally regenerated in-situ multiple times, significantly reducing replacement costs and waste generation.

Customizable Shape

Blocks, cylinders, panels, and custom geometries available to match your existing reactor or duct dimensions.

Low Dust Generation

Monolithic structure eliminates attrition fines common in granular carbon beds, keeping downstream equipment clean.

Applications

Widely used across multiple industries

VOC Abatement

Removal of volatile organic compounds from paint spray booths, printing, and chemical plant exhaust streams.

Industrial Odor Control

H₂S, NH₃, and mercaptan removal in wastewater treatment plants, landfills, and rendering facilities.

Indoor Air Purification

Large-scale HVAC integrated purification for commercial buildings, clean rooms, and hospitals.

Catalyst Support

Honeycomb monolith as substrate for catalytic coatings in DeNOx, CO oxidation, and ozone decomposition.

Solvent Recovery

Concentration and recovery of solvent vapors in chemical, pharmaceutical, and coating industries.

Automotive Cabin Filter

Aftermarket and OEM cabin air purification systems for removal of NOx, SO₂, and particulate matter.
