Pellet Activated Carbon

Home / Products / Pellet Activated Carbon

Overview

Pellet (Extruded) Activated Carbon is manufactured from coconut shell and coal-based raw materials extruded into cylindrical shapes. The most commonly used diameter is 4mm, with options from 0.9mm to 8mm.

With high hardness, low pressure drop, and excellent adsorption kinetics, pellet activated carbon is the preferred choice for gas phase applications, continuous processes, and high-flow systems.

Diameter: 0.9mm - 8mm
CTC: 20 - 80%
High hardness & low dust
Custom lengths available

Product Features

Low Pressure Drop

Uniform cylindrical shape minimizes resistance in packed bed systems.

High Mechanical Strength

Excellent wear resistance for long service life in continuous systems.

Gas Phase Excellence

Optimized for VOCs, H₂S, and mercury vapor adsorption.

Regenerable

Can be thermally reactivated multiple times with minimal losses.

High Bed Uniformity

Consistent particle shape allows even flow distribution.

Diameter Options
