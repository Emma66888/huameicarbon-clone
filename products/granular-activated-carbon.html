Overview

Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) is produced from high-quality coconut shell and coal-based materials. With its porous structure and large surface area, it is ideal for adsorption applications in both liquid and gas phases.

Available in a wide range of mesh sizes and iodine values, our granular carbon meets the most demanding requirements for water treatment, gold recovery, and industrial purification processes.

Mesh sizes: 4x10 to 30x60
Iodine value up to 1200 mg/g
Coconut shell & coal based options

Product Features

Why choose our granular activated carbon

High Adsorption

Large surface area (900-1300 m²/g) for superior adsorption capacity in both liquid and gas phases.

High Hardness

Excellent abrasion resistance, minimizing fines generation during handling and backwashing.

Regenerable

Can be thermally reactivated multiple times with minimal loss of adsorption capacity.

Low Soluble

Excellent chemical stability with very low solubility in water and organic solvents.

Custom Mesh

Various mesh sizes available from 4x8 to 30x60 to suit your specific application.

Pure Quality

Low ash and impurity content, meeting food grade and drinking water standards.

Applications

Widely used across multiple industries

Drinking Water Treatment

Wastewater Treatment

Industrial wastewater decolorization and organic pollutant removal.

Gold Recovery

Gas Treatment

VOCs removal, H₂S elimination, and air purification.

Purification of pharmaceutical intermediates and API production.

Food & Beverage

Decolorization and purification of sugar, oil, wine, and beverages.
