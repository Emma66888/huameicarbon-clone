Granular Activated Carbon

Coconut shell & Coal based granular activated carbon. Available in multiple mesh sizes for water cartridges, wastewater treatment, gas treatment, mercury removal, food & beverage, gold recovery, and pharmaceuticals.

Pellet Activated Carbon

Coconut shell & Coal based cylindrical activated carbon. Diameter 0.9-8mm, most commonly 4mm. High activity for VOCs, H₂S, and natural gas mercury removal applications.

Powdered Activated Carbon

Coconut shell, wood & Coal based powder. Various fineness for water treatment, sugar/oil decolorization, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, and waste incineration gas treatment.

Honeycomb Activated Carbon

Innovative form with large contact area and developed micropores. Low wind resistance, high adsorption capacity. Ideal for high-flow, low-concentration VOC purification.
